About Me

Hey! My name is Bradley Caravana, I'm based out of Long Island, New York, and I'm currently a student with an eye for web development. In addition to devoting a great deal of my time to web devlopment, I've built a number of computers that are used for many different purposes, ranging from a complete workstation, to an email and web browsing machine; on my spare time, I take timelapses/photos with my GoPro Hero 5 camera.

My goals with this website are to showcase my web development skills and to layout the work I've completed in my career thus far. I have experience working with SASS (the styling for this portfolio is written in SASS), CSS frameworks such as Bootstrap and Materialize, and frontend JavaScript.

If you're interested in my work, get in touch with me using the contact form below. In the meantime, explore the site and stay awhile!
